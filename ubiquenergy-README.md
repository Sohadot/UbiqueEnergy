## Core Thesis

**The Problem:**

Energy systems are undergoing the most fundamental transformation in a century:

- From centralized generation → distributed generation
- From passive consumption → active intelligence
- From isolated infrastructure → converged energy-information networks

**But:** No one has defined the conceptual framework for this transformation.

Existing energy discourse is trapped in industrial-era thinking. Smart grids, DERs, microgrids, V2G—these exist, but there is no **unified topology** that makes them intelligible.

**What UbiqueEnergy Does:**

UbiqueEnergy defines the **conceptual foundation** for understanding energy systems as distributed topology, not commodity flow.

It answers:
- What is energy as topology?
- How does distributed intelligence work?
- What happens when energy and 6G converge?
- How do you design grids that are both resilient and intelligent?
